Services

From initial groundworks to final as-builts, we support your programme with dependable data, clear communication and punctual attendance.

01

Construction setting out

Grids, foundations, holding-down bolts, 278 Section Roads, steel frame buildings, RC frames, drainage, kerbs & paving.

02

topographic surveys

Detailed level & feature surveys with spot levels, breaklines and contours, supplied in CAD & PDF.

03

As-built surveys

Record surveys for QA and handover, including concrete pours, steelwork, roads and utilities.

04

monitoring

High-precision deformation monitoring for structures, retaining walls and earthworks.

05

measured building surveys

Floor plans, elevations and sections for refurbishments and extensions.

06

control and grid establishment

Primary/secondary control, GNSS baselines and traverse adjustment with clear reports.
